Earth Renewed - Lichen Flower

Index

The website puts you into the perspective of an alien that has crash landed on to the planet Earth to find out the human race has disappeared and this mysterious moss has covered the planet.

My concept behind this project was to create a world where nature has “reclaimed” the land where cities once stood. During the quarantine, there were instances where the streets were so barren that animals were roaming the empty streets of once busy cities. I thought it would be interesting to create a familiar setting that has been transformed by a natural force, which brings in the concept of “world-building”. I have created a world that does not exist in the present but a world that contains scenery and landscape that is familiar yet new to the viewer. I would be interesting to push the idea further and add more abstracted concepts such as now that humanity has disappeared from the planet the essence of the earth can truly manifest of something of that nature.
