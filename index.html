Earth Renewed

The Aftermath of Humanity - Moss Land

You are an alien that has crash-landed on the planet “Earth” that was once populated by “humans”. However, the human race has since perished and all that is left is this mysterious moss that covers all evidence of industrialized human civilization. This moss has mysterious properties and glows a mysterious blue. You take care of documenting this new lichen life-form with the observations below.
